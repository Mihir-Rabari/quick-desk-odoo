Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password? ‚ñº

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click "Forgot Password?"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Check your email for reset instructions
  5. Follow the link in the email to create a new password
How do I update my profile information? ‚ñº

To update your profile:

  1. Go to the Profile page from the navigation menu
  2. Click on "Personal Info" tab
  3. Edit the fields you want to change
  4. Click "Save Changes" to update your information
How does the search feature work? ‚ñº

Our search feature allows you to:

  • Search across all content types (documents, users, projects, files)
  • Filter results by category
  • Sort results by relevance, date, or name
  • View highlighted search terms in results
How do I manage notifications? ‚ñº

To manage your notifications:

  1. Go to Settings from the navigation menu
  2. Find the "Account Settings" section
  3. Toggle email and push notifications on/off
  4. Or visit the Notifications page to see all your notifications
What browsers are supported? ‚ñº

Our application supports all modern browsers:

  • Google Chrome (latest version)
  • Mozilla Firefox (latest version)
  • Safari (latest version)
  • Microsoft Edge (latest version)
